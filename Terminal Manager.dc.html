1Wireline — terminal manager with an AI Operator

A WindTerm-style workbench rebuilt in the Broadsheet system: paper chrome, serif labels, cyan for interaction, magenta reserved for the AI Operator's identity. The only dark surfaces are the terminals themselves. The Operator lives in a right-hand panel attached to the focused terminal, and also appears in the session tree as a session type of its own — an agent-driven device, like an SSH host. Modes: Manual (every command needs approval) and Auto (runs with a full ledger).
